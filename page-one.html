Explore

In Barcelona, you can visit many iconic landmarks like La Sagrada Familia or choose to immerse yourself in the fervor of Camp Nou, home to FC Barcelona.

La Sagrada Familia

Antoni Gaudí's unfinished masterpiece, is an awe-inspiring basilica renowned for its extraordinary architecture and intricate details, captivating visitors with its ongoing construction and spiritual grandeur.

Where do I find it?

To reach La Sagrada Familia, utilize Barcelona's efficient public transportation, such as metro lines L2 and L5. Alternatively, take a leisurely stroll or use buses that serve the area, and head towards the Eixample district.

For the Football Enthusiasts

Camp Nou is the iconic stadium located in Barcelona, Spain, and is the home ground of FC Barcelona. Take a tour, shop the merchandise, embrace the atmosphere.

For the Shop-aholics

Barcelona's high street shops, like Passeig de Gràcia and Portal de l'Àngel, offer a vibrant and diverse shopping experience with a mix of local boutiques and international fashion brands set against the city's bustling atmosphere.

For the beach lovers

Barcelona's beaches allure with golden sands, vibrant energy, and the Mediterranean's gentle waves. Lively beachfront promenades offer a blend of sunbathing, water sports, and iconic views of the city's architecture.
