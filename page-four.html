Accommodation

Barcelona offers a diverse array of places to stay, ranging from luxurious beachfront resorts to charming boutique hotels.

Stay

Hostels, apartments, and beachfront resorts cater to varied preferences. Whether nestled in historic neighborhoods or along the bustling avenues, they offer diverse amenities and local charm.

Top three hotels

Mandarin Oriental, Passeig de Gràcia

A central location on Passeig de Gràcia makes Mandarin Oriental the epitome of luxury. A stay at the hotel features opulent accommodations, Michelin-starred dining, a tranquil spa, and personalized service.

The One, Calle de Provença

There are chic rooms with Wi-Fi, smart TVs and minibars, as well as suites with cathedral views and terraces with hot tubs. Upgraded suites include a separate living area and a kitchenette. There is a 24-hour concierge service option.

Gran Hotel Calderón, Rambla de Catalunya

The modern rooms feature free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs, Nespresso machines, minibars, and views of the city. Suites feature separate living rooms, while upgraded rooms have balconies or patios. There is 24-hour room service available.

Top three hostels

Primavera-Hostel, Calle de Mallorca

Featuring original brick ceilings, the bright dorms offer free Wi-Fi, lockers and balconies with city views. Bathrooms are shared. Private rooms are also available. The hostel offers complimentary tea and coffee. Common areas include a kitchen and a colorful lounge. There are guest computers.

The Loft Hostel, Calle de Provença

Featuring light-wood design accents, the modest dorms provide bunk beds, storage cupboards and free Wi-Fi, as well as shared bathrooms. Some are gender-segregated, and private rooms with en suite facilities are available. Towels are not provided.

Hostal Poblenou, Carrer del Taulat

Featuring high ceilings and colored mosaic floors, the casual rooms come with en suite bathrooms, free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs, and tea and coffeemakers. Some include balconies; 1 has a kitchenette. Breakfast is served in a quaint patio courtyard that has exposed stone walls. Pets are welcome with prior notice.
